About Gyrix

The Mission of Gyrix is to set 500 entrepreneurs on a path to success by building a strong technical foundation. We do it by providing world class technical service through consultative approach, technical prowess and partner-like mindset. We believe in up-skilling, hard work and continuous improvement. Value addition to our clients, stakeholders, team members, partners, environment and society at large is at the heart of our mission while making profits and having fun.

Headquartered in Central India, we focus on empowering businesses globally to help them stand out from the crowd by providing them tailored solutions to fit their business needs. We take ownership of our work and ensure proactive steps with a focus on building long-term relationships with our clients.

Why we do it

  • To make the world a better place through innovative technology solutions
  • To harness the potential of SMEs and SMBs
  • To generate more value through large enterprises
  • To convert startup ideas into reality
  • To demystify technology even for a kid

Our story

Started as a casual chat between two High School students, the idea of being an entrepreneur came to Rashmirathi Tiwari during the school’s summer break. The idea stayed with him and he focused on acquiring the required skills of being an exceptionally talented business person. He worked at a job for a few years and learned the required skills with the help of his colleagues. Finally in March 2012, with the help of his wife, Arti Tiwari and ex-colleague, Kunal Bhusare, they faced it all whatever came their way and established “Gyrix Technolabs”.

Gyrix - The Name

With an intention to create an organization that makes innovative technological solutions and a culture that supports, attracts, and nurtures happy brains; we named ourselves Gyrix derived from the medical term “Gyri”. Gyri is plural to Gyrus which represents a ridge on the human brain.
