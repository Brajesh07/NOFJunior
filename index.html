About

Toonofy Global Pvt Ltd.:Toonofy is an education technology company delivering the engaging learning experiences to students across the globe. Toonofy is the world's only interactive, gamified and animation based International Olympiad examination powered with Artificial Intelligence.
We have mapped the entire syllabus into animated stories.
The questions are interactive, with voice support which means the students can touch the cartoons, and other elements on the screen, read & listen to the question as well.
Our revolutionary technology identifies individual student learning gaps and fills those gaps through the algorithmically generated analysis report & the remedial plans. Students from Play Group to 5th can participate.
